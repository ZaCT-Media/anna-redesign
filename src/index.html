About Me

Hello, I’m Anna! Cooking & Baking have always been my passion. What started as a hobby in my kitchen has blossomed into a small business right here in Leicester. My goal is to bring joy to your celebrations with delicious, beautiful, and unforgettable bespoke creations.

Quality is everything. I source the best local ingredients, support sustainable farming, and put care into everything i make.
